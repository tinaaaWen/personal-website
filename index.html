Funny Things You May Want to Know

Lucky? Unlucky? I'm lucky. I did not do well in the high school entrance examination, but because my grades were still very outstanding at my junior school, I got into the best high school in our province (Henan, one of China's most populous provinces). My last semester of high school coincided with the COVID19 epidemic, and I received many offers but was stranded for a year because the embassy couldn't get me a visa. My parents let me enroll in the national college entrance examination before, so I was lucky enough to enter a Chinese university to study Landscape Architecture for one year. At the end of July 2020, I received an offer from Virginia Tech, which came late, but fortunately I had not yet come to the U.S. I dropped offer from Penn State and then accepted the offer from Virginia Tech that I had longed for.
