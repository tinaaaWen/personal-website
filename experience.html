Professional Experience

Below are some highlights of my professional experiences in computer science, including a summer internship, research involvement, and class projects.

Team Leader, Duck Pond App Project, 01/2024 - 05/2024

I organized brainstorming sessions to create the prototype using Sketch and Figma, and conducted user testing to improve the app's usability. Feedback was analyzed through a UX Target Table, which helped iteratively refine the app.
